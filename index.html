Book Cricket is a game of scoring runs. Decide on the number of batsmen and the book you want to play with and then hit start. Rifle through the pages of the book and select a place to open the book. The last unit of the page number on the even numbered side determines the outcome. For instance, if the page opened is 154, the last unit is 4 and you score 4. 2,4 and 6 will be added to the batsmanâ€™s score and 8 will be counted as a single. 0 means the batsman is out. Stay away from the cover page of the book to avoid getting out but try to stay within the book. After playing a ball, hover the mouse on the red square to close the book and face the next ball. The innings will go on until all the batsmen are out. The second player then takes over the book and continues to play. At the end, whoever has the higher score wins!
